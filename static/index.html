Can an AI read
the fine print?

Every food package sold in the US must carry a Nutrition Facts label that follows 40+ exact FDA rules — specific rounding increments, ingredient ordering by finished weight, calorie math from Atwater factors, minimum type sizes based on package area. Getting any of it wrong is a federal violation.

This protocol injects deliberate errors into a draft label, then deploys an AI agent to find and fix every mistake — without being told which fields are wrong. The agent revises up to 10 times to reach 90% compliance.
